About Becoming A Chef

A Tribute To A Dream, I've Been Awaken From By COVID-19

When in 2018 my Carreer as an observatory engineer at the Vienna Planetarium came to a sudden end, It didn't take more than a weekend to decide, where the future would lead me. I would - with the very much appreciated collaboration from my Parents and Sisters - open a restaurant in the former Wine Cellar of my first home, the cottage in Zagersdorf I grew up on.

Food Design at the New Design University in St Pölten and the Vienna Vocational School, Internships at Ottakringer, and Habibi und Habara woul occupy me for the next two years.

But personal development and Investment Know How would be an important topic too. And with time these would become increasingly important. The COVID-19 Pandemy eventually administered the final stab to my idea of Becoming A Chef.

This is an hommage to a great idea, that lead me very far, without having to be carried out until the initial goal.
