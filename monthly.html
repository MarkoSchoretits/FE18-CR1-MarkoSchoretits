Which flowers are edible?

Let’s do a little edible flowers 101 ~ spring is the perfect season to find and use colorful edible flowers as a garnish in special dishes because by the time summer rolls around many of them have come and gone. Below is a list of the more common edible flowers so you’ll know what to look for. Any of these colorful spring blooms would be perfect for a salad like this. Here are some you’re likely to come across.

  • carnation
  • chrysanthemum
  • citrus blossoms
  • cornflower
  • dandelion
  • day lily
  • dianthus
  • daisy
  • geranium
  • hollyhock
  • honeysuckle
  • impatiens
  • jasmine
  • lavender
  • lilac
  • marigold
  • nasturtium
  • pansy
  • passionflower
  • clover
  • rose
  • snapdragon
  • sunflower
  • violet
